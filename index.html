I, Mark Bragg, am the owner of Bragg Builds LLC. I enjoy writing apps and helping others learn to create on their own.
I currently have one product in market:
Push. Pull. Legs.

Bells and Whistles

I like to write simple apps that serve a sole purpose. For me, most apps do too much, and I find the extraneous features to be more burdensome than anything. To be honest, I don't like a large portion of apps simply because they're bloated with extra features.

Ads

My apps have advertisements to support my business. If you want to get rid of the ads, I provide the ability to do so through a small transaction.
